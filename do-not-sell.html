Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to opt out of the sale of their personal information. Roofing Estimate Now (roofingestimatenow.com) may share certain information with our marketing partners in ways that qualify as a "sale" under the CCPA. Use the form below to submit your opt-out request and we will process it within 15 business days.

Your Rights Under the CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

What Information We May Sell

We may share with third-party marketing partners the following categories of information you provide when requesting a roofing estimate:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Service address and zip code
  • Project type and details (roof type, scope of work)
  • IP address and browser/device identifiers collected via cookies and tracking technologies

This information is used to match you with licensed roofing contractors and may be considered a "sale" under California law even when no money changes hands.
